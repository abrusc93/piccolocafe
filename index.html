Piccolo Cafe was established in 2017 and was created by the DeThomasi family for the community to have a relaxing place to go and unwind. Our menu includes fresh baked goodies, imported Italian gelato, paninis, salads, cappuccinos, lattes, healthy organic smoothies, and much more! We wanted to provide a menu that would be healthy and sinful at the same time, for any type of mood that you're in.
